About Our School

Madhya Vidyalaya Usari, Biraul, Darbhanga was established in 1961 and has been a pillar of quality education for the local community for over six decades.

Operating under the Bihar Government's Education Department, the school is committed to holistic development of students through a structured curriculum, dedicated faculty, and a nurturing environment.

Under the able leadership of Headmaster Shri Lalit Kumar and a team of 12 dedicated teachers, the school strives for academic excellence and character building.

Admissions for the new academic session 2026–2027 are now open. Contact us today!
